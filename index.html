What is our target technology?

For our project, we have decided to take a focus on generative AI, specifically the technology ChatGPT, developed by OpenAI. It is built on the Generative Pretrained Transformer, or GPT architecture. This architecture allows it to understand language, context, and tone. It is a state-of-the-art large language model, capable of generating human-like text based on the user's input, allowing it to perform tasks such as having conversations, math, coding, and numerous other daily tasks. It represents a major advancement in natural language processing, allowing for more interactive, intelligent, and accessible communication between humans and machines.

What is the importance?

The importance of ChatGPT revolves around it’s cutting edge capabilities alongside its rapid growth in modern society .As it has grown, it has spread to numerous sectors of society and industries such as customer service, education, health-care, and numerous parts that may utilize the integration of AI to gather quick solutions that may not take great precedence on an organization or individuals needs.

ChatGPT's Application Domains

  • Customer Service: Many businesses use ChatGPT to power chatbots, handle FAQs, and manage basic support tickets. This reduces wait times and offloads repetitive tasks from human agents.
  • Education: ChatGPT is used for tutoring and academic support. It assists students with writing, explains complex math concepts, summarizes texts, and offers quick feedback on assignments. Its 24/7 availability makes it especially useful for remote or underserved learners.
  • Healthcare: While not responsible for diagnoses or treatment decisions, ChatGPT can summarize patient notes, generate reports, help assess symptoms, and simplify medical instructions. It is used as a supportive tool, though not widely adopted for direct medical use.
  • Software Development: Developers use ChatGPT to generate small code snippets and debug issues. This helps streamline development and reduce time spent on redundant tasks.
  • Business and Data Analytics: Professionals in finance, marketing, and data science use ChatGPT to draft emails, create summaries, generate reports, and interpret data, helping them better understand business trends.

These application domains highlight how ChatGPT has evolved from an experimental AI to a practical tool embedded in professional and educational environments. While it doesn’t replace human decision-making, it serves as a valuable assistant in many tasks.
