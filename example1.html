Example 2: ChatGPT used in educational apps and services like Duolingo and Quizlets

Apps like Duolingo and Quizlet use ChatGPT to create interactive lessons and study tools. This allows students to practice conversations, get feedback, and review material at any time

While it raised concerns about cheating at first, many schools now explore how AI can support learning, not replace it.
