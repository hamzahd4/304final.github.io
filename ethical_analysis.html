Ethical analysis and social implications: What makes the use of technology complicated and debatable?

What makes the use of ChatGPT and AI controversial and complex is it involves many risks especially to us, we run the risk of losing our jobs, our reputation being tarnished, even our ethics going out of the window, and even critical thinking skills not being used. It threatens some of our most important human values. It is complicated due to the benefits and harm it makes it an ethical dilemma.
